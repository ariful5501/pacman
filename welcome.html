Welcome to the game Pacman

  1. Objective: The objective of the game is to control the Pacman character and eat all the food dots while avoiding the ghosts.
  2. Controls: Use the arrow keys (left, up, right, down) on the keyboard to control the movement of Pacman.
  3. Pacman: Pacman is represented by a yellow circle. It moves through the maze and eats food dots. The direction changes based on the keys pressed by the player.
  4. Ghosts: Ghosts are represented by colored rectangles. They move randomly through the maze and try to collide with Pacman. If Pacman collides with a ghost,
    the player loses a life.
  5. Walls: The maze contains walls represented by blue rectangles. Pacman cannot move through walls.
  6. Food Dots: Food dots are represented by small rectangles scattered throughout the maze. Pacman can eat these food dots by moving over them.
  7. Lives: The player starts with three lives indicated by Pacman icons at the bottom of the screen. The game ends when all lives are lost.
  8. Score: The player's score is displayed at the top left corner of the canvas. Each food dot eaten adds points to the score.
  9. Game Over: The game ends when Pacman loses all lives. A "Game Over!" message is displayed on the canvas, indicating the end of the game.
